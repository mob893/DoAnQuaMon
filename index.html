Definitions

Definition of environment

The environment consist of 2 elements: The natural environment – where everything biotic or abiotic occur naturally - encompasses the interaction of all living species, climate, weather and natural resources that affect human survival and economic activities. The build environment are where humans have fundamentally transformed landscapes such as urban settings and agricultural land conversion, the natural environment is greatly changed into a simplified human environment. Though many animals build things to provide a better environment for themselves, they are not human, hence beaver dams and the works of mound-building termites are thought of as natural.

Types of pollutions

There are many types of pollutions such as: water, earth, air, light and noise. Whilst water, earth and air pollution can occur both in natural and build environment; light and noise pollution purely come from humans.
