The status of light pollution

Light pollution is a kind of pollution that is solely artificial due to the overwhelming amount of light created that make it turns detrimental to the environment and to human ourselves. Light polution comes from many sources however, in our opinion, the main cause is from urbanization as we consider light as an everyday necessity.

It has been estimated that 83 percent of the world's people live under light-polluted skies and that 23 percent of the world's land area is affected by skyglow. Studies show that urban areas are more at risk. Globally, it has increased by at least 49% from 1992 to 2017, also a new study published in Science reports that between 2011 and 2022, global sky brightness increased by an estimated 9.6% per year.
